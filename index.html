Why Choose Us

What is Abacus?

Abacus is a powerful brain development program that helps children improve calculation skills while strengthening concentration, memory, logical thinking, and confidence. As children progress, they learn to perform calculations mentally by visualizing the abacus.

How Does It Work?

Children begin with a 1:4 Soroban Abacus and gradually develop the ability to solve calculations mentally through regular practice. This improves speed, accuracy, focus, and problem-solving skills.

Why Does It Help Brain Development?

Abacus combines logical thinking with mental visualization, encouraging both sides of the brain to work together. The result is sharper thinking, better concentration, stronger memory, and greater confidence in learning.

"More than Mental Math — A Foundation for Lifelong Learning."

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Abacus?

Abacus is a brain development program that improves calculation skills while enhancing concentration, memory, logical thinking, and confidence.

Who can join?

Our program is designed for children aged 5 to 14 years, with learning tailored to each child's age and ability.

How many classes are held each week?

Classes are conducted 5 days a week (Monday to Friday) through engaging, structured learning sessions.

Will it help in school?

Yes. Abacus strengthens focus, memory, speed, and logical thinking, helping children perform better across all subjects—not just Mathematics.

Does my child need to be good at Mathematics?

Not at all. Abacus is designed for every child. No special maths skills are required—children improve naturally through regular practice and guided learning.

Is Abacus only about fast calculations?

No. Faster calculation is just one benefit. Abacus also develops concentration, memory, visualization, logical thinking, confidence, creativity, and problem-solving skills.

Will Abacus increase my child's study burden?

No. Abacus is designed to make learning easier. Many children become more focused, complete homework faster, and feel more confident in the classroom.

Is Abacus useful only during childhood?

No. The skills developed through Abacus—such as concentration, analytical thinking, confidence, and faster learning—benefit children throughout their academic journey and future careers.
